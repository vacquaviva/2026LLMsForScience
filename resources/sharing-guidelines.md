# Sharing Guidelines

This repository is useful for sharing course materials, but it is best to be careful with copyrighted readings and slide assets.

Good options:

- Link to library resources, publisher pages, open-access copies, or course reserves.
- Upload materials you created yourself.
- Upload public-domain or openly licensed materials and keep license notes nearby.
- Avoid uploading scanned chapters, paywalled PDFs, or copyrighted slides unless you have permission.

For a private class repository, you may still need to follow your institution's copyright and fair-use policies.
